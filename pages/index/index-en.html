History

ADDEVSOFT is a 100% Colombian company, founded in 2023.

Its founders: Anderson Vera and Duvan Hernandez. Two developers with one extensive experience in web development and passionate about technology.

They worked together on different projects for several years of freelance form. Projects that included template development in HTML & CSS, WordPress, WooCommerce, among others technologies.

In November of 2022 the idea of creating ADDEVSOFT arises, as a technological solution that offers different services easily and quickly to his clients.

Finally in February 2023 ADDEVSOFT is released to the public.
